About Us

Welcome to Gigablast (Yash Book Store House)

Located in the heart of Latur, we have been serving book lovers for over a decade. Our mission is to provide the best collection of Indian literature and motivational books at affordable prices.

We carefully curate our collection to include both classic titles and the latest releases across various genres. Our knowledgeable staff is always ready to help you find your next great read.
